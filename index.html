Akan Names Generator

This is a web application that calculates the day of the week you were born and then depending on your gender it outputs your Akan name.
After submitting your date of birth and gender, you will get an alert of your day name in Ashanti Twi,a dialect of Akan and most widely spoken language in Ghana.
The table on the left side shows akan names of male and female according to the day of the week they were born.
